About Us

We are a small team of four programmers inspired to find students the programming help they need throughout their learning course. Through our survey, we found that programming students often have trouble learning and perfecting the way that they code based on the classes they go through alone. As such we have developed an e-learning platform, Code4Student that aims to provide reliable tutoring and learning resources to students.

Objectives

Reinforce Students’ Programming Knowledge and Skills

Using this system, novice programmers will be able to receive guidance and solutions to their problems through a reliable source.

A Source of Practical and Industrial Knowledge

As we plan to hire experts in the field of programming to be as mentors, mentees will benefit from the mentors’ working experience and knowledge. This also allows mentees to discover insights about the working industry related to programming.

To Help Tutors with Their Career Profile

If our system is recognised by employers, a mentor’s reputation and expertise can be included in their CV as a form of additional experience.

Reduces the Workload of Lecturers

With the system in use, the number of students seeking for a lecturer’s help will be reduced. Lecturers will have more time to prepare their teaching materials, therefore providing an effective learning environment for the students.

Increase the Overall Prestige of the College

Mentees will have better programming insights from the mentor’s expertise, thus preparing themselves to work under reputable companies when they graduate. As a result, companies would recognize INTI that produces promising programming graduates.
